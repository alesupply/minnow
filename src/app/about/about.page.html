About Minnow POC A Proof Of Concept (POC) idea that we have discussed internally is the ability to open a Locker unit by approaching it and pressing a button on a mobile device to open a given location. This would open up thought processes and potential development opportunities into other areas that would allow us to serve a different purpose to regular inventory control applications. For the POC we would want to be able to walk up to the machine with a mobile, connect with it over low-power WIFI to the Minnow-board, and enter a hardware address in the form of PIC, Drawer and Compartment and press a button on the mobile device to open the matching physical address on the Locker. The Minnow-board would need to take that data and formulate a packet to send the command over the standard RS-232 serial bus that we use to open the corresponding location.
